About HYDRA-X

HYDRA-X is a revolutionary peer-to-peer electronic cash system that allows you to send money over the internet, without any restrictions. HYDRA-X is decentralized, open source, and gives users full control over their money. HYDRA-X is fast, efficient, and secure, backed by industry leading technology and security.
